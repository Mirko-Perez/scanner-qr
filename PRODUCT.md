# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Two roles use this system during a live quinceañera event:

- **Staff / scanner**: operates the QR check-in at the entrance, needs fast confirmation at a glance under mixed indoor/outdoor lighting, often holding a phone with one hand.
- **Organizer / admin**: manages the event setup beforehand (tables, guests, QR generation) and monitors arrival progress in real time from a laptop or tablet at the venue.

Both are time-pressured during the event itself. Mistakes or delays are visible to guests.

## Product Purpose

ScannFest is a QR-based check-in and event management system tailored for a quinceañera. It handles guest registration, table assignments, QR code generation, arrival scanning, and a shared memories/photo gallery. Success means zero friction at check-in and full arrival visibility for the organizer throughout the evening.

## Brand Personality

Elegant, composed, efficient. The product should feel like it belongs at a special occasion — refined enough for the context, but never decorative at the expense of clarity.

## Anti-references

- Glamorous party-supply aesthetics: stickers, confetti, loud colors, sparkle effects
- Generic SaaS dashboards that feel corporate and cold
- Anything that distracts from the primary task (confirm arrival, see who's missing)
- Nightclub-neon color schemes

## Design Principles

1. **Function first, elegance second** — every screen has one primary action; decoration is subordinate to it.
2. **Glanceable at a glance** — status, counts, and confirmations must be readable in under 2 seconds.
3. **Calm confidence** — the UI should not feel urgent or alarmed; even errors should feel managed.
4. **One context at a time** — don't mix event-night scanning UI with admin setup UI; each surface has a clear role.
5. **Earned restraint** — elegance comes from what's removed, not what's added.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Staff may use the scanner in dim or bright outdoor conditions; contrast ratios must hold up. Touch targets sized for rushed single-hand use (≥44px).
